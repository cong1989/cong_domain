About Us

Sky Smash Academy is dedicated to helping kids aged 4 to 18 improve their badminton skills and overall fitness. Our goal is to provide a friendly and supportive environment where young athletes can thrive.

Programs

Location: SSSS

Time: Every Friday, 3:30 PM to 5:15 PM

Age Group: 4-18 years

We offer comprehensive training programs tailored to different skill levels. Our programs focus on skill development, fitness, and fun!
